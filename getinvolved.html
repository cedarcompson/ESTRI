Get Involved

ESTRI is a living field institute shaped by the communities and collaborators that make it possible. Whether you are a researcher, supporter, or simply curious, there are ways to participate in the ecosystem we are building together.

Ways to Connect

  • Reach Out: We welcome connection, questions, and conversation. You can email us or use our contact form to get in touch.
  • Research Opportunities: Learn more about our internship program (launching Fall 2025) and our upcoming field residencies (Summer 2026). We’re building ethical, supported pathways for embedded researchers.
  • Community Partnership: We're always looking for intentional communities to partner with as we develop our embedded community research program. Find out more here.
  • Support the Work: Help sustain our infrastructure through Open Collective. Any donations we receive go directly towards funding our developing internship and embedded researcher programs.

We believe in slow growth, consentful expansion, and emergent partnership. If something in this work resonates with you, we invite you to step closer.

We are building something new. And there’s room for you here.
