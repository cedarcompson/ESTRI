Relational Systems Theory

Everything is made of relation.

At the core of our framework is the understanding that systems are not things—they are interactions. This theory draws from complex systems thinking, liberation psychology, family systems, and transdisciplinary philosophy. We explore how power, communication, and feedback loops shape the ecologies we move through.
