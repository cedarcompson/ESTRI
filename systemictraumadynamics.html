Systemic Trauma Dynamics


Survivorship as framework, not footnote.

This work investigates how trauma moves through systems: across individuals, collectives, infrastructures, and institutions. We trace how origin wounds shape cognition, communication, and the body politic. This includes explorations of plurality, group trauma, inherited harm, and how we build beyond survival.
