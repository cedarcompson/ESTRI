Infrastructure Disclosure

ESTRI is currently hosted via GitHub Pages with domain services provided by GoDaddy. We do not endorse or align with these platforms’ broader practices, and we maintain only minimal reliance to ensure accessibility in our early development stage.

We explicitly refuse integration with additional third-party services. Upon domain renewal in 2026, we will transition to Njalla for registrar support, aligning with our values of anonymity, autonomy, and decentralization.

Our goal is to self-host all digital systems, archives, and communications by 2028—on our own hardware, land, and terms.
