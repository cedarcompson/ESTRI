Field Notes

Relational ethnography from within.

This is where we document what it means to be in the system—not outside of it, not abstracted from it. Field Notes are live records of emergent observation: autoethnography, communal witnessing, structure-mapping, and the soft architecture of daily survival. Noticing is a form of labor. Noticing is a form of care..
